About the Project

Welcome to the Quick Weather App, a project developed by Smita Dutta and Manab Seal as part of the "Fundamentals of Java and Web Programming" course at Anudip Foundation. This app allows you to quickly check the weather using the OpenWeatherAPI, providing you with accurate and up-to-date information at your fingertips.
